  • Fire Fighting Appliances

    Louis Safety offers Inspections and Annual Maintenance to your firefighting and personal safety equipment to help you reduce the risk of damage to your vessel, injuries to your crew and customers, and fines from fire code noncompliance.

    Services

    Portable and Wheeled Fire Extinguishers, Breathing Apparatuses (BA) and Emergency Escape Breathing Devices (EEBD), Medical Oxygen Resuscitators and Cylinders, Immersion Suits and Inflatable Life Jackets, Fixed CO2 Systems, Wet Chemical Systems, Foam Systems and Foam Applicators.

    We ensure all equipments are:

    Properly serviced and certified, Appropriate for the specific hazards in your facilities, Fully charged and ready for immediate use, Guaranteed compliance with national, state and local requirements.

  • Liferafts

    Our liferaft service station is equipped with the latest technological equipment and operates in accordance with international approvals and governance registrations. Our service team follows regularly liferaft training courses with fully qualified instructors that are held either in our station or in international training centers to ensure that they are educated in all aspects of liferaft servicing.

    All necessary testing is performed to your liferafts in order to ensure they can function regardless of the weather conditions, and a wide range of brand-approved spare parts is offered to ensure your liferafts include only the proper relevant equipment.

    Approvals

    Our station and our technicians are certified to service a big variety of liferaft brands such as YOULONG, CSM, and others.

  • Lifeboats

    We offer you annual and five-year periodic inspections and tests on lifeboats, rescue boats, free-fall lifeboats and their launching appliances such as davits and winches, according to current legislation as per MSC1206/rev01.

    Repairs

    Additionally we provide you with any kind of repair. Our fully experienced and qualified technicians are available 24 hours / 7 days.

  • Automation

    We have engineering, control & instrumentation specialists. We are involved in the design, service, supply, repair, maintenance and upgrade of instrumentation, electrical and process control systems, including the provision of specialist engineering and commissioning manpower.

    Offers
    We service a wide range of industries including the offshore, marine, LNG, power, utilities, petrochemical, storage and processing industries.
