Liferaft Inspection

Our liferaft service station is equipped with the state of the art equipment and operates in accordance with international approvals and governance regulations. Training is a part of our quality process and our service team undergoes continual development training either in-house or international training centers by qualified experts in the field.

All required testing is performed on liferafts in order to ensure they can function regardless of the weather conditions. A wide range of respective OEM approved spare parts are offered to ensure the equipment respond on demand.

Our facility and personnel are certified to service a range of liferaft OEMs; YOULONG, CSM to name a few
